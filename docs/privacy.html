Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 5, 2025  ·  Last updated: May 5, 2025

The short version: Sideout is a private, offline-first journaling app. Everything you write stays on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any of your personal information. No accounts, no cloud sync, no ads.

1. Introduction

Sideout ("we", "our", or "the app") is a volleyball journaling application developed for Android. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information. Please read it carefully. By using Sideout, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or process:

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials
  • Your journal entries, notes, or any written content
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or analytics
  • Location data
  • Usage statistics or crash reports sent to our servers
  • Payment information (the app is free)
Sideout has no backend server, no user accounts, and no network communication of any kind. The app functions entirely offline.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

All data you create inside Sideout — including journal entries, mood selections, tags, context labels, and app preferences — is stored exclusively on your device in a local database (Isar). This data never leaves your device.

Your data is protected by your device's operating system sandbox, which prevents other apps from accessing it. Only you can read, edit, or delete your journal entries within the app.

4. App Permissions

Sideout does not request sensitive system permissions. The app requires only the standard permissions needed to store data locally on your device:

  • Storage: Used solely to read and write your journal entries in the app's private directory. No access to photos, shared storage, or files outside the app.

5. Third-Party Services

Sideout does not integrate any third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or social login. We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data to share.

The app is distributed through the Google Play Store. Google's own privacy policy governs any data Google collects as part of operating the Play Store. Sideout itself does not contribute any user data to Google beyond what Google already collects through the Play Store distribution.

6. Children's Privacy

Sideout is suitable for users of all ages, including minors. Because the app collects no personal information from anyone, it naturally complies with children's privacy regulations including COPPA and GDPR-K.

7. Your Data Rights

Because all data resides only on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: All your entries are readable inside the app at any time.
  • Edit: You can edit any entry at any time.
  • Delete: You can delete individual entries within the app, or delete all data by uninstalling Sideout.
  • Export: There is no data held by us to export or request.

8. Data Security

Your journal data is protected by the Android OS application sandbox. Access requires physical possession of your unlocked device. We recommend using your device's built-in lock screen for additional protection.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the app's features change in a way that affects how data is handled (for example, if a future version adds optional cloud backup). Any material change will be announced via an app update notice, and the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised.

Continued use of Sideout after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
